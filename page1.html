About us

Beamish Cog Railway,
situated in the beautiful grounds of the award winning visitor attraction, Beamish Museum. 

Our miniature railway was founded in 1991.
The railway is extremely unique as we are proud to boast the only 7 1/4 inch gauge rack railway in the United Kingdom.
Brainchild of the profound engineer, Ken Swan, the rack railway is a feat of engineering and it make for an extremely intresting, if not thrilling, journey.
The railway is run by soley by volunteers, and we keep the railway running by asking visitors for a small donation.
We hope you can have a ride sometime soon!
